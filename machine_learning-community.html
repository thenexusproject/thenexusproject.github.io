ML Community

Welcome

Introduction

Welcome to The Nexus, Machine Learning community, by being a part of our community you have essentially taken the first step towards becoming a fully fledged ML expert.

At the ML community we learn through peer-to-peer learning and teaching, so that you feel at rest and at ease while learning this advanced topic which might seem intimidating at first glance but becomes really fun and enjoyable to learn with your peers.

Our Aim

To make sure that by the end of college every members should be familiarized in the field of AI and machine learning, it is not necessary to have mastery on the subject, the aim is to have fun while learning it.

  • Get basic to advanced level knowledge in ML
  • Work in Arificial Neural Networking
  • Create projects using ML and AI
  • Be eligable to apply for works that require skills in ML
